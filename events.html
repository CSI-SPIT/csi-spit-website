SCRATCH | September 2017

This workshop was conducted for the first year students.In the two hours the students were made familiar with the basics of SCRATCH and were also taught how to develop logic when given a problem. They were taught the drag and drop system of SCRATCH and were taught the if-else and looping constructs in scratch.At the end the CSI team guided them to develop a simple game which everybody enjoyed alot.



Hacking Bad | September 2017

The members of CSI-SPIT and ITSA organized 'Hacking Bad', a 24-hour AI Hackathon, which was a pre-event for the technical festival, Matrix, of the college. An attendance of over 16 teams (around 64 students) was observed. Students belonging to reputed engineering colleges from all over Mumbai, like VJTI, DJ Sanghvi, Fr. Agnel etc participated. Our problem statements covered a wide range of domains like natural language processing, image recognition etc which was given by Cere Labs. Cere Labs even provided the participants with pre-trained models.



Codehousie | August 2017

This competition was conducted for SEs of Computer and IT branches. This was conducted to make students familiar with the coding competitions and to prepare them for competitive coding. The problem statements were hosted on Hackerrank so that the students get acquainted with the Hackerank environment.The competition was given the format of the popular party game Housie so as to make it exciting.



Git and GitHub | July 2017

This workshop was conducted for SEs and TEs Computer and IT branches n collaboration with ITSA. The students were made familiar with the uses and the commands of Git by Aashish Nehete from TE IT and the functionalities of Github were taught by Parth Tamane from TE IT. Topics covered were commands in Git such as add, commit, push, how to create repository and use of GitHub.



Treasure Hunt | July 2017

This was an ice-breaker event organized for FEs as it was the start of the year. It was organized by CSI-SPI, FACE and ITSA. Since the FEs are all new and have got no idea about the college and the campus this event aimed at making the FEs familiar with the campus. It aimed at them exploring the campus and knowing the campus and college better. Some of the spots selected by us were the Amphitheater, Seminar hall, Yoga club, etc.
